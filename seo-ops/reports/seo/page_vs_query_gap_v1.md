## Limitations

1. **GSC data covers 90 days** with 3-day lag; low-volume queries may be underrepresented
2. **Position data is averaged** and may not reflect current rankings
3. **Theme/intent classification is heuristic-based** with simple pattern matching
4. **Cannibalization detection uses 3+ pages threshold** which may overcall for broad terms
5. **No external SERP data** (competitor analysis, SERP features, etc.)
6. **Page mapping relies on page_inventory v1** which may miss dynamic/redirected URLs
7. **GA4 engagement metrics** may not align perfectly with SEO performance window
8. **Planner volume estimates** are rough and may not reflect actual Dutch market demand
