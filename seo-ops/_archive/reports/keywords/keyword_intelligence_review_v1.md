## 7. Risks and cautions

- **Low Ads data volume:** 30-day Ads window with limited conversions makes PPC conclusions provisional.
- **Brand distortion:** Brand queries excluded from most analyses but some edge-case brand terms may remain.
- **Heuristic limitations:** Theme, intent, and mapping classifications are rule-based, not ML-verified.
- **Mapping uncertainty:** Low-confidence mappings are best guesses. Manual verification recommended before acting.
- **GSC position averaging:** 90-day averaged positions may not reflect current rankings.
- **Theme taxonomy mismatch:** keyword_master uses `core_isolation`, page_inventory uses `gevelisolatie` — some cluster analysis may show false mismatches.
- **Planner-only keywords:** High planner volume without GSC/Ads evidence does not guarantee opportunity — SERP analysis needed.
- **No competitor intelligence:** Cannot assess whether opportunities are realistic without SERP feature/competitor data.
